# default style

Neutral, informative, section-headed. No hype, no filler. Suitable as
a starting point for general blog posts, newsletters, and knowledge
articles.

**Status:** stub. v0.1 populates this with concrete rules derived from
the private reference rulebook (stripped of platform assumptions).

## Rules (WIP)

- Preserve the speaker's original argument structure.
- Remove verbal tics, repetition, and sponsor breaks.
- One topic per section; descriptive H2 headings.
- Short paragraphs (2–4 sentences).
- No "in conclusion" / "in this article we will" framing.
